    • #2 matthew's white nintendo

    Description

    Creating a lost object.

    Matthew's Lot Object:

    A white Nintendo OLED switch Joy-Con Grip with two Joy-Con on it.

    There are rounded rectangular panels and four buttons and a joystick on each side. rounded corners on one side and flat on the other side.

    Reflection

    At first, I was not at all clear about what object I was making. Thankfully, my partner gave me some additional verbal instructions that helped me understand the basic form of a nintendo switch. One main takeaway I had while experimenting with the software was that if a circle or line does not show up write away, you can adjust the grid and position your object into the part of the axis you want it to be in. It also a lot of playing around with numbers and calculating distances.
