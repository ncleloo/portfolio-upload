    • #3 parameterized face

    Description

    In parametrics, planes that created faces, objects, and 2D surfaces are all coherent to the axis. This face is generate to be abstract, yet distinct, and by clicking the creen, viewers are able to interact with the face and enlarge or decrease the size of its features.

    Design Process

    I designed this parametric face with the intention to dismantle the idea of beauty. Simple changes such as "clicks", "shifts", and coding errors can easily change the appearance of a face. So, I wanted to question the idea of making adjustments to physical beauty. It is admirable how easy it is that make physical changes to make one feel better, but, Are we really changing that much?

    Reflection

    This project was a turning point for me because I was usiing the idea of physicality to create somehting digital. Ultimately I realized the physical realm and the digital realm work simultaneously, and the coding got easier too.
